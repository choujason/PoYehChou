    About

    周伯燁 Po-Yeh Chou.

    National Cheng Kung University
    Tainan, Taiwan

    I'm Po-Yeh Chou, major in Industrial design in National Cheng Kung University. I'm working in Cognition, eXperience & Behavior Design Lab (CXB Design Lab) since I was sophomore. In CXB lab, I focus on behavior change of pro-environmental behavior. “Green Action” is one of my research, which added plants to be as the mechanism for changing human’s behavior. My projects are mainly related to Human-Computing Interaction (HCI), which includes User-Experience design(UXD) and User Interface design(UID).

    Specially, all my projects are related to People, Technology and Design. I believe that understanding people is the first steps to develop the useful but warm solution. Technology can provide the novel vision for us to explorer the unknown possibilities. And Design, is way to spread the solution for those in need, in the world.
